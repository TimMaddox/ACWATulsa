Welcome to the Academy! We are an independent branch of the A.C.W.A. located in Tulsa, Oklahoma. We are pleased to be able to offer you real personal defense solutions that can work for everyone, anytime, anywhere!

Here's what you can expect to find when you train with us:

  • Real Solutions
  • This is not a sport; we are not interested in points or competitions, just what works in the real world for real people like you.



    •  
  • Fast & Efficient
  • In a "time is life" situation, seconds count. A.C.W.A. Combatives is designed to give you the most efficient solutions in the smallest amount of time.



    •  
  • Force Continuum
  • We believe that force must parallel threat. With that being the case, we need to be able to be flexible in our responses and choose the most appropriate action for a situation. Not every problem is a nail, not every solution is a hammer!



    •  
  • Instinct
  • Our materials are easy to learn because they work with your natural protective instincts, not against them (like many arts do).



    •  
  • Safety
  • Our only goal is to get you and your loved ones home safe each and every night. It's as simple as that.



    •  
  • Join The Family
  • If you are looking for a home away from home where you can feel like you're part of something special, then you have found the right place. We take great pride in providing a unique environment, free of EGOs, that is conducive to learning.
