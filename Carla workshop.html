Move Your DNA Workshop

Instructor: Carla Harless

When: October 6th & 7th

Cost: $289

Biographical info:

  • For over 10 years, Carla Harless was a certified yoga teacher but felt there was something missing from her understanding of how the body moves & functions. She also was experiencing chronic migraines, a niggling lower back pain, along with a myriad of other discomforts/dysfunctions. Her thirst for knowledge, endless curiosity, and firm belief that she was not doomed to a life of pain just because she was getting older lead her to Nutritious Movement and the work of Katy Bowman. This program/system/philosophy blew her mind and vastly improved her quality of life. Carla earned her Healthy Foot Practitioner certification then dove deeper by becoming a Nutritious Movement, Certified Restorative Exercise Specialist.
  • Carla is also a Licensed Massage Therapist & YTU® Therapy Ball Practitioner…she believes that movement & massage are the best tools for health & vitality.
